Edge Cases:

Mathematical Logic:
- Upper triangle: For row i (1 to n), spaces = n-i, stars = 2*i-1
- Lower triangle: For row i (n-1 to 1), spaces = n-i, stars = 2*i-1
- Total rows: 2*n-1 (upper + lower triangles, middle row counted once)
- Perfect symmetry achieved through identical space calculations

Time Complexity: O(n²) - nested loops iterate proportionally to n²
Space Complexity: O(n²) - pattern string size grows with n²
